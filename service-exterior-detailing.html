Service Overview

Our Exterior Detailing service is designed to bring back the showroom shine to your vehicle. We use premium products and proven techniques to clean, protect, and enhance your car's exterior surfaces.

From hand washing to paint protection, every step is performed with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring your vehicle looks its absolute best.

Our Process

1

Pre-Wash Inspection

We thoroughly inspect your vehicle and document any existing damage or areas of concern.

2

Wheel & Tire Cleaning

Deep clean wheels, tires, and wheel wells to remove brake dust and road grime.

3

Hand Wash

Gentle hand washing using the two-bucket method with premium pH-balanced soap.

4

Clay Bar Treatment

Remove embedded contaminants for a smooth-as-glass finish.

5

Wax & Protection

Apply premium carnauba wax for lasting protection and brilliant shine.

6

Final Touches

Dress tires, restore trim, and perform final quality inspection.
