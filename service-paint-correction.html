Service Overview

Paint correction is the process of removing imperfections from your vehicle's paint surface through multi-stage machine polishing. This service eliminates swirl marks, light scratches, oxidation, and other defects that diminish your paint's appearance.

Our certified technicians use professional-grade equipment and techniques to safely restore your paint's clarity, depth, and gloss without compromising the clear coat.

Correction Process

1

Paint Inspection

Assess paint condition and measure thickness to determine safe correction levels.

2

Thorough Wash & Decontamination

Complete wash and clay bar treatment to remove all surface contaminants.

3

Compounding Stage

Remove heavy defects with cutting compounds and aggressive pads.

4

Polishing Stage

Refine the finish with medium polishes to remove compounding marks.

5

Finishing Stage

Final polish with finishing pads for maximum gloss and clarity.

6

Protection Application

Apply protective sealant or wax to preserve the corrected finish.
